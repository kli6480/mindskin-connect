For Clinicians

MindSkin Connect is a digital home for the Mind-Skin Health for Atopic Dermatitis (MSHAD) patient education modules, developed by the University of Alberta Division of Dermatology. The platform is designed for adults and older teens managing their own eczema, and is shared with patients via QR code at participating clinics.

The platform delivers five modules:

  • Module 1a — Understanding Your Skin-Mind Connection
  • Module 2 — Breaking the Itch-Scratch Cycle
  • Module 3a — Building Your Self-Management Skills & Confidence
  • Module 4 — Building a Support Network
  • Module 5 — Holistic Skin Care and Wellness

Each module ends with patient-voice prompts ("Bring this to your appointment") that patients may choose to raise during follow-up visits. These are designed to support shared decision-making, not to direct it.

For questions about the platform or the underlying MSHAD program, please contact the program team at supportg@ualberta.ca.

This site does not collect personal information.
